## GUI corrections to the DRM Plus Application

===========================================

1. Changed "Reference" field label to "Model" in Device Tracking to match the text populated in Progress Notes.
2. Added legal boilerplate and additional text to the "About..." screen as per VA requirements.
3. Added check and filter for control characters in Speed Codes.
4. Added catch to prevent errors for potential duplicate incomplete D2940 transactions.
5. Updated "Panel Add/Edit" screen to allow for partial panel moves, removal of deceased patients from panels, and changed Fee Basis to Integrated Veteran Care in provider table.
6. Corrected an error when completing CPRS templates.

## M corrections to the DRM Plus Application

=========================================

1. Added a check for a patient to have a Date of Death and filter for reports.
2. Adjusted verbiage for PCE data filing in confirmation message.
