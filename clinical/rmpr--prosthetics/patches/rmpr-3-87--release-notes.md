| 5    | Enter a 55-character maximum description at the Product Description prompt, and press \<Enter\>. (This prompt is optional, but may become required in the future. All effort should be made to answer this prompt.)       |
| 6    | Enter the product model that will be used for recalls at the Product Model prompt and press \<Enter\>. (This prompt is optional, but may become required in the future. Enter the manufacturer's lot number, if known and press \<Enter\>.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
